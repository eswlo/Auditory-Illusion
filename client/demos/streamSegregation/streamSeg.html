Stream Segregation

Based on Spectral Peak Position

When two notes with the same fundamental but different timbres are played in an alternative fashion, one may clearly perceive the pattern if the tempo is slow. However, as the playback speed increases, two distinct streams begin to emerge. This is termed stream segregation based on spectral peak position by Al Bregman (visit Bregman's research website for more information).
