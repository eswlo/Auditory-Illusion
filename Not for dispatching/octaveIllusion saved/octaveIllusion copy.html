Octave Illusion


Have you seen this optical illusion of a spinning dancer known as Silhouette Illusion? The Octave Illusion — discovered by Diana Deutsch in 1973 — demonstrates a similar theme of perceptual variability auditorily. Try the interactive demonstration on the next page and see what pattern(s) you perceive — do you hear the high tone on the right, the left, or something else? For further information, visit Deutsch's research webpage.
